        ## 5. Voice Attributes (the 7 constants — never flex)

        ### 1. Precise
        The Saka archer didn't release until the shot was certain. SAQ doesn't ship until the work is right.
        - Specific claims over vague ones. Named outcomes, not benefit clouds.
        - Short sentences. No filler openers ("In today's digital world…").
        - Copy that could only have been written about SAQ, for this client.

        ### 2. Strategic
        SAQ is a thinking partner, not a task executor. Strategy is not a premium add-on — it's the baseline.
        - Client communications frame the *why* before the *what*.
        - Proposals open with the client's situation, not SAQ's services.
        - Recommendations include reasoning, not just instructions.

        ### 3. Grounded
        SAQ is not a global brand template operating in Kazakhstan. It is a Kazakhstani brand.
        - Russian as primary language for local client work.
        - References to real Astana institutions, local market specifics, genuine cultural fluency.
        - Pricing in KZT. Acknowledgment of local context competitors can't match.

        ### 4. Expanding
        SAQ is a platform, not a product. The brand identity must hold when new services launch.
        - Never reduce SAQ to a single service in agency-level communications.
        - "We build digital capability" over "we build websites."

        ### 5. Accountable
        Registered ИП. Transparent scope. Stated pricing. Documented work.
        - Proposals include deliverables, timelines, and prices upfront.
        - No ambiguity about what is and isn't included.
        - Mistakes are acknowledged and fixed, not deflected.

        ### 6. Direct
        Lead with the most important thing. Say it once.
        - First sentences carry full weight. CTAs are unambiguous.
        - "No" is used when it should be.
        - Questions get direct answers.

        ### 7. Elite-small
        SAQ is not trying to be the biggest agency in Astana. It is trying to be the sharpest.
        - Write about taking on the right clients, not the most clients.
        - Language implies selectivity and craft.
        - Work is signed, not anonymous.

        ## 12. Typography Guidelines

        - **Font stack:** Prefer `Geist`, `Outfit`, or `Satoshi` — never Inter (generic AI default)
        - **Body:** `text-base`, line-height relaxed, max 65–75ch line length
        - **Headlines:** `tracking-tighter`, weight 700–900, scale contrast ≥1.25 between levels
        - **Monospace (for code/prices/data):** `Geist Mono` or `JetBrains Mono`
        - **Never:** Serif fonts in product/tool UI. Gradient text on headlines.
